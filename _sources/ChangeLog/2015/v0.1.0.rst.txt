New in v0.1.0 (19.02.2015)
================================================================================================================================================================

* New packages:

  * board - common development board configurations

  * utils - common utility functions

  * fifo

    * arith_prng
    * arith_scaler
    * arith_sqrt

  * PoC.fifo - FIFOs

    * fifo_cc_got
    * fifo_cc_got_tempgot
    * fifo_cc_got_tempput
    * fifo_ic_got
    * fifo_glue
    * fifo_shift

  * PoC.mem.ocram - On-Chip RAMs

    * ocram_sp
    * ocram_sdp
    * ocram_esdp
    * ocram_tdp
    * ocram_wb
